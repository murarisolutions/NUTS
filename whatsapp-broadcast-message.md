## How to Use

1. Open `http://localhost:8000/whatsapp-ad.html` in browser
2. Screenshot each of the 4 images separately (they are 1080x1080 — perfect for WhatsApp/Instagram)
3. In WhatsApp Business → Broadcast Lists → New List
4. Send Image 1 + Message 1 first
5. Wait 1-2 days, then send Image 2 + Message 2
6. Wait 1-2 days, then send Image 3 + Message 3
7. Send Image 4 + Message 4 to anyone who hasn't ordered yet
8. Use the Quick Follow-Up for personal replies
